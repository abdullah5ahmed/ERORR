BMW I8

The BMW i8 is a hybrid sports car from the German manufacturer BMW . The car has two engines; The first operates on gasoline, located in the rear, consisting of three 1.5-liter cylinders with a twin turbocharger, while the second engine works on electricity located in the front, bringing the total power of the car to approximately 362 hp and 570 Nm of torque. The BMW i8 has four driving modes; The first mode is E-Drive , which depends entirely on the electric motor and can drive the car for a distance of 35 km using this mode at a speed not exceeding 120 km / h. The second mode is Eco Pro , which has the same characteristics as the E-Drive with a slight difference, as the mode aims to save oil in the maximum degree. 3rd position ComfortIt combines the work of the electric and gasoline engines, and the fourth mode Sport provides the most powerful performance possible for the car.
